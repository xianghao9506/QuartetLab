## Overview
The **Megatron XL** is a high-voltage electrostatic headphone amplifier designed for demanding Stax-type headphones, including **SR-009**, **SR-X9000**, and compatible models.

It prioritizes stability, low noise, and long-term reliability while maintaining a transparent and dynamic presentation.
